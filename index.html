About Pollution

Pollution is the introduction of harmful materials into the environment. It affects air, water, and soil, and poses serious risks to human health and ecosystems.

Main Causes of Pollution

  • Industrial emissions
  • Vehicle exhaust
  • Plastic waste
  • Deforestation
  • Improper waste disposal

Effects of Pollution

On Humans

Air pollution causes respiratory diseases, heart problems, and other health issues.

On Wildlife

Animals suffer from habitat loss and poisoning due to pollution.

On Climate

Pollution contributes to global warming and unpredictable weather patterns.

Solutions & What You Can Do

  1. Reduce, reuse, and recycle materials.
  2. Use public transport or carpool.
  3. Support clean energy initiatives.
  4. Plant trees and support green spaces.
  5. Educate others about pollution prevention.
